About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission at Victory Women FC is to combine the benefits of soccer with mental health support, empowering women and young girls in Ottawa to achieve their soccer dreams while cultivating resilience and emotional well-being.

History & Inspiration

Victory Women FC was founded with a simple idea: to create a space where women and young girls can chase their soccer dreams while receiving mental health guidance and support. We recognized that mental resilience is just as important as physical fitness, and through our program, we aim to address both, helping women and young girls thrive in life and sport.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: We inspire women and girls to believe in themselves, both on and off the field.
  • Mental Well-being: Through workshops, mentorship, and counseling, we help our players strengthen their emotional health.
