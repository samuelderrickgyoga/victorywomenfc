Our Programs

Soccer Training & Development

  • Weekly soccer training for skill development, teamwork, and fitness.
  • Opportunities to participate in local competitions, tournaments, and matches.

Mental Health Workshops

  • Regular sessions led by mental health professionals to support emotional well-being, teach stress management, and build resilience.
  • One-on-one counseling available to help players cope with personal challenges.

Dream Mentorship Program

  • A mentorship initiative where older players or coaches guide younger girls in setting and achieving their soccer and life goals.
  • Special guest speakers from the soccer and mental health community to inspire and motivate.
