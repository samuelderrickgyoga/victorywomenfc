Welcome to Victory Women FC!

Victory Women FC is a nonprofit soccer team empowering women and young girls in Ottawa. We foster mental health and resilience through soccer, helping women and girls develop soccer skills while nurturing mental well-being for a brighter and successful future.

Get Involved

Vision & Goals

At Victory Women FC, we believe in the power of soccer to transform lives. We provide a supportive environment where girls can pursue their soccer dreams while receiving mental health support, boosting confidence, and enhancing emotional strength.
